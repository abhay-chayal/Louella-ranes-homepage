The Author

Louella Ranes

"I enjoy creating the world of Yiddera and getting to know the people who live there."

Louella Ranes is an epic romantic fantasy writer whose imagination has built a world spanning five continents and eight centuries of magic, love, and consequence. Her stories weave together ancient prophecies, forbidden connections, and the eternal dance between demons and mortals in the world of Yiddera.

When she is not writing, Louella enjoys family time with her husband, son, and two dogs. She is also passionate about painting, reading, and learning about different plants and their many uses — inspirations that breathe vivid life into her richly detailed world.

A figure whose presence ripples through the entire series, Tayin stands at the crossroads of fate and duty in the world of Yiddera. Their choices shape the course of entire continents and the lives of those they love.
